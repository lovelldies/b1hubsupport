title: How to access Shock Sensors?

1. Tap on**My Devices**on your Home page.

2. Select '+' icon on your screen. Select **Security and Safety**.

    ![]({{ site.img }}/64XCoroaDpY1487969184268-untitled.jpg)

3. Then, select **Shock Sensors**.
