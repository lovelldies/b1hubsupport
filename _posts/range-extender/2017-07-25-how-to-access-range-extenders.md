title: How to access Range Extenders?
---

1. Tap on **My Devices**.

2. Select '+' icon on your screen. Select **Security and Safety**.

    ![]({{ site.img }}/JVGh7MXQ1XQ1487966450107-untitled.jpg)

3. Then, select **Range Extenders**
