Step 2: Select **Fibaro Roller Shutter 2**. In order to pair the device with the Hub, tap the **Next**Â button on the bottom of your screen.

Step 3: Click the black button on the rear of the switch thrice.![]({{ site.img }}/image33.png)

Step 4: Enter the device information. Tap **Done**.
